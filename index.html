Dacapo Welcomes You

To The Piano Academy & Art Gallery

Dacapo is a piano academy and art Gallery with the aim to enhance the piano community in Kuwait. Dacapo offers piano classes to individuals of all levels (beginner, intermediate, advance.) The other side of Dacapo is the art gallery, where the artist can show their art on the academy walls.
